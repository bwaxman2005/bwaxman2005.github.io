As of March 27th our photovoltaic system has generated 406 kWh of useable electricity. Considering how few days of sunshine we have had in St. Louis, this certainly meets our expectations. The AC output of the panels was 15 kWh or more on at least three days. Then on March 23, with nearly full sun 15.7 kWh of AC was generated. Both our installer, Missouri Solar Living, and our local electric utility, AmerenUE, have been very good to work with. Ameren was very prompt in sending out our rebate check and they installed a new digital meter about one month ago. They did charge for the new meter, but we were advised of this by our installer before we started the project. We are anxious to see how the system will perform as the days get longer with more days of sun. If anyone would like additional information, please send me an email and I will be happy to answer any questions that I can.
