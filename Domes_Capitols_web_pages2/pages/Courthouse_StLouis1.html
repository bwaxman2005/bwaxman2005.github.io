Courthouse_StLouis1

This photograph was taken with a 10.5 mm fish-eye lens. It shows the interior and the dome of the
Old Courthouse in St. Louis MO. This courthouse is most famous for the Dred Scott Case in
1847 and 1850. For more information see the National Park Service Website.

www.nps.gov/archive/jeff/courthouse.html
